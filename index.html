Research Interests

Everything I work on circles one theme: agents that can be trusted to do real work. Concretely, that breaks into three threads:

  • Training environments — RL environments and automated task generation that exercise real software skills, not toy benchmarks.
  • Data for agents — dataset pipelines, validation, and privacy tooling for agent-training corpora at scale.
  • Evaluation & reliability — rubric-based grading, and measuring when an agent's output can actually be trusted.
